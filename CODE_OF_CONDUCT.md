# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Standards

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Being respectful and constructive.
- Accepting feedback gracefully.
- Prioritizing the community and project goals.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include harassment, personal attacks, and discrimination.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.
